A real orchard. Straight from the tree.

MangoBerryEstate has been farmed by the same family for four generations. We grow Langda, Dussheri and Chausa mangoes, fresh litchis in early June, and rotate seasonal vegetables in the kitchen garden — okra, tomatoes, brinjals, gourds, leafy greens.

Booking a slot reserves a guided basket for your group. Our team walks you through the rows, shows you which fruits are ready, and weighs your harvest at the gate.
